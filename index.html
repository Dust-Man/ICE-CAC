The Avarage Contextual Chingo

The Avarage Contextual Chingo (ACC) is a unique unit of measurement based on the Mexican expression "chingo", which is used to describe a large amount of something, but always within a specific context. A "chingo" is highly subjective and depends on the situation in which it is used—whether it's the number of tortillas eaten, tasks completed, or anything else.
